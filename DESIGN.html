THE DIRECTION

Cave-painting pixel × carved stone × firelight

This is your caveman mode plugin made visible. Claude shows up as a literal caveman — pixel, bushy-browed, grunting through a carved stone tablet. The skin leans into prehistoric materials (rock, bone, hide, torchfire) instead of chrome and neon. Boldness lives in one place: Caveman Claude and his stone-tablet dialogue. Everything else stays dark and readable, because you still have to read code on a small screen.

He's a presentation layer, not a new personality — same Claude, fewer syllables. Your ooga mode gets the nod: short words, big thoughts.

THE CHARACTER

Caveman Claude

An original 20×24 pixel caveman — wild hair, heavy brow, beard, fur hide, bone pendant. Boxed beside the dialogue, JRPG-style.

States, all from data you already emit:

  • Idle — blinks in his tablet. The torch-glow border = bridge connected (fire lit).
  • Talking — mouth flaps "OOGA" while a new assistant line types in.
  • Thinking — brow furrows, tablet shows ME THINK… until the first transcript line.
  • Away — desaturated when the session is exited (fire went out).

MOTION

Deliberate, not everywhere

  • Entrance — dialogue types in (Fredoka), mouth flaps OOGA, then a blinking ▼.
  • Idle — he blinks every few seconds. The torch glow flickers low in the background. That's it.
  • Ambient — rock grain + firelight vignette + faint handprints. No moving scenery.
  • Respect prefers-reduced-motion — blink, flap, and torch flicker all freeze.
